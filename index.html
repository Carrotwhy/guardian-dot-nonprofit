Safety that stays with the people you love.

GuardianDot is a nonprofit initiative providing affordable GPS safety devices and real-time location sharing for seniors, children, and individuals who need an extra layer of protection.

How It Works

A small wearable or stationary device, a live map, and the people who care — all connected.

1

Wear or Place the Device

Each GuardianDot device is registered to a person — worn on the body or placed in a fixed location like a home.

2

Track in Real Time

Location updates stream live to a map, so caregivers always know where their loved one is.

3

Get Alerted Instantly

A press of the help button — or an automatic fall detection — sends an immediate alert to every connected caregiver.

Built for Peace of Mind

Every feature exists to answer one question fast: are they safe?

📍

Live GPS Tracking

See a device's last known location on a map updated in real time, with history of recent movement.

🆘

One-Touch Help Button

A single press sends an immediate "help requested" alert to everyone watching over that device.

🤕

Fall Detection

Wearable devices can detect a fall automatically and notify caregivers without anyone needing to act.

👪

Caregiver Sharing

Owners can share access to a device with family members or caregivers, who get the same real-time visibility.

🏠

Wearable & Stationary

Use a wearable device for someone on the move, or a stationary unit to monitor a fixed location like a home.

🔒

Private by Design

Only the device owner and the people they explicitly share with can see a device's location and alerts.

Our Mission

GuardianDot exists because safety shouldn't be a luxury.

We believe every family should have access to reliable, affordable tools that help keep their loved ones safe — whether that's a child walking home from school, a senior living independently, or someone managing a health condition that puts them at risk. As a nonprofit, we provide devices, app access, and support at little to no cost to the families who need it most, funded by donations and community partners.
